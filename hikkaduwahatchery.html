Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

About the Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a conservation center located in Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka. It plays a crucial role in protecting sea turtles and their eggs, as well as educating visitors about these fascinating creatures.

The hatchery works closely with local communities and volunteers to ensure the safety and well-being of sea turtles. It serves as a sanctuary for injured and orphaned turtles, providing medical care and a nurturing environment for their rehabilitation.

Additionally, the hatchery actively engages in turtle conservation efforts by conducting research, monitoring nesting sites, and releasing hatchlings into the ocean. Visitors have the opportunity to witness these releases and learn about the hatchery's vital conservation work.

Its Conservation Efforts Presently, there are five species of turtles that are taken care of in the hatchery and rescue centre. Such species are the Olive Ridley Turtle, Leatherback Turtle, Loggerhead Turtle, Hawksbill Turtle, and Green Turtle. The facilities of the sea turtle hatchery and rescue centre are all state-of-the-art, complete with water tanks to care for newborn turtles, along with adult turtle species that have been rescued from the sea due to injuries. One of the more peculiar sights in the centre is the area in which the turtles lay their eggs, as they have distinct little mounds of sand piled neatly between one another. Each of these mounds is monitored closely to calculate the hatching process of the turtle eggs.

Fun Activities

Apart from learning about sea turtles and their conservation, the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery offers various activities for visitors to enjoy:

  • Guided Tours: Take a guided tour of the hatchery to gain insight into the lifecycle of sea turtles and their conservation.
  • Turtle Feeding: Get a chance to feed turtles and learn about their diet and feeding habits.
  • Turtle Release: Participate in the release of baby turtles into the sea, contributing to their survival in the wild.
  • Educational Programs: Join educational programs and workshops organized by the hatchery to deepen your understanding of sea turtle conservation.
  • Souvenir Shop: Visit the on-site shop to purchase souvenirs, handicrafts, and items that support the hatchery's conservation efforts.
